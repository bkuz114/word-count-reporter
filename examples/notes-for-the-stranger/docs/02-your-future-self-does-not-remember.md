# Your Future Self Does Not Remember

You think: *I will remember why I wrote this.*

You will not.

Six months from now, you will stare at your own code like it was written by a stranger. You will ask questions you cannot answer. *Why this function? Why this name? Why did I think that edge case mattered?*

Documentation is not for other people.

It is for you, six months ago, who thought memory was stronger than it is. It is for you, six months from now, who will be grateful for a single sentence that saves twenty minutes of confusion.

The kindest gift you can give your future self is a note that says: *I was here. I was thinking about this. Here is what I learned.*

Your future self will never thank you. But they will notice.
