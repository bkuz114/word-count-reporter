The Person Who Never Thanks You

You will never meet the person who benefits most from your documentation.

They will not file an issue. They will not send a pull request. They will not star your repository or reply to your thread or buy you a coffee.

They will simply not have a problem. They will read your note. They will understand. They will move on.

That is success. The absence of confusion. The avoided question. The hour they did not lose.

It is hard to feel good about something you never see. But try, anyway. Imagine a stranger, somewhere, nodding at their screen. Not frustrated. Not stuck. Just... moving forward.

You did that. You will never know. And that is fine.
